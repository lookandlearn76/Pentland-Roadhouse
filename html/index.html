About us

We're a family run bar and restaurant where you can come with your family for a nice relaxing time. We have a function suite, beer garden, kids play area and a large car park, all with disabled access.

We're like to think of ourselves as a bar with a big heart. We give to the community as best we can and regularly donate to our favourite charities.
